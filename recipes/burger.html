Burger

Description

A juicy, flavorful burger made with simple ingredients and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 for best flavor)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 4 burger buns

Steps

  1. Prepare Patties: Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Shape into 4 evenly-sized patties, pressing a slight indentation in the center of each.
  2. Cook Patties: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes per side, adding cheese slices during the last minute if desired.
  3. Toast Buns: Lightly toast burger buns on the grill or in a pan until golden.
  4. Assemble Burgers: Place patties on the bottom bun and layer with desired toppings. Add the top bun.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with your favorite sides like fries or salad.
