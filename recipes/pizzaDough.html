Pizza Dough

Description

Make soft, fluffy pizza dough in no time with this simple recipe. Perfect for a homemade pizza night!

Ingrediants

  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Activate Yeast: Combine yeast, warm water, and sugar in a bowl. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Mix Dough: In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, stirring to form a dough.
  3. Knead: Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Let Rise: Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  5. Shape: Punch down the dough, roll it out, and shape it into a pizza crust.
  6. Bake: Add toppings and bake at 475°F (245°C) for 12-15 minutes.
